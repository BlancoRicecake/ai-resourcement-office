# DECISIONS

Approved / held / rejected / repeat-apply decisions from discovery work.

| Date | Decision | Status | Rationale |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| — | (e.g. "exclude reseller accounts for target X") | approved / held / rejected | (reason) |

## Principles

- Record which thresholds, exclusions, and channel choices were approved, held, or rejected, and
  why.
- A rejected direction is not re-proposed (unless new evidence or a context change is stated).
- A repeat-apply rule (e.g. "for this target, always exclude MLM handles") is recorded here and in
  `USER.md`.
- Disagreements about a lead's role classification or fit threshold and the final agreed call are
  logged here.
- If a decision generalizes, flag it as a promotion candidate for a `knowledge/` node (via
  `RUNS.md`).
