# Destination bootstrap

## Status

- `researching`: identity or official source fields are incomplete. Do not calculate a final route.
- `provisional`: identity and starting sources exist, but costs, inventory, and place facts require verification.
- `validated`: provider mapping, representative place data, routing assumptions, and failure behavior passed tests.
- `unavailable`: safety, legal, or data limitations prevent a responsible plan.

## Research order

1. Resolve country, city, airport codes, currency, timezone, and coordinates.
2. Add Korean foreign-ministry and destination-government entry and safety sources.
3. Add official tourism, airport, public-transport, road, parking, and fuel sources.
4. Check flight, stay, place, route, and rental provider capabilities.
5. Record taxes, fees, seasonality, holidays, crowd patterns, accessibility, and emergency contacts.
6. Attach source URL, status, observation time, validity period, and recheck need to each changeable fact.

If web search is unavailable, leave tasks pending and provide official or comparison links. Never claim automatic collection. Do not promote a destination to `validated` until fixed-fixture tests cover missing fields, stale sources, provider failure, costs, hours, and route feasibility.
