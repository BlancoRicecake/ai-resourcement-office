# Korean traveler checklist

Use this checklist for Japan and United States trips. Verify current rules at response time; do not encode visa-free status as a permanent fact.

## Before recommending purchase

- Confirm the passenger names and dates without collecting passport numbers.
- Confirm baggage allowance on the exact fare family.
- Confirm self-transfer, airport change, overnight layover, and minimum connection risk.
- Confirm accommodation occupancy, bed configuration, taxes, resort or destination fees, and cancellation deadline.

## Before departure

- Link to the Korean Ministry of Foreign Affairs Overseas Safety Travel service: https://www.0404.go.kr/
- For Japan, link to the Embassy of Japan in Korea and Visit Japan Web: https://www.kr.emb-japan.go.jp/ and https://www.vjw.digital.go.jp/
- For the United States, link to the U.S. Embassy in Korea and official ESTA service: https://kr.usembassy.gov/ and https://esta.cbp.dhs.gov/
- Ask the traveler to verify passport validity, entry authorization, onward ticket, travel insurance, medicines, customs restrictions, and emergency contacts.
- State the date checked and make clear that final admission is determined by border authorities.

## Local presentation

- Show Korea time for departure coordination and local time for the itinerary.
- Show KRW totals and supplier currency side by side.
- Explain tipping, taxes, destination fees, and transport passes when applicable, marking unverified amounts clearly.
